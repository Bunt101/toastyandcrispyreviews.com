Welcome to the reviews page! Here you can find my reviews of movies, books, and video games.

Reviews are a great way to express yourself and share your opinions!

A lot of people use the 'letter grade' ranking or use numbers out of 5 or 10. I like to rank things out of 4 stars. I'm a big fan of Roger Ebert and his ranking system has always stuck with me. In a way, my usage of the 4-star ranking can be a dedication to one of the greatest critics out there.

Most reviews are written by me, but occasionally, I allow others to contribute. I will credit you, so don't worry about that. If I wrote the review, I wouldn't include that. So, Don't be a coward and share your opinion with the world!
